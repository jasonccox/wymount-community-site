Service Ideas

Are you looking for a way to be of service in the community? Here are some ideas of how to do that.

  • Sweep out your stairwell (or someone else's if you're feeling extra kind)
  • Introduce yourself to your neighbors
  • Walk around and pick up any trash you see
  • Offer help when you see someone moving
  • Shovel snow from your stairwell or other walkways (the grounds crew will plow the fire lanes)

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I'm locked out?

The Wymount Terrace Office (located in the Multi-Purpose Building) is open from 8:00am-8:00pm Monday-Friday and can lend you a key. If the Wymount Office is closed, the Heritage Central Building Office can lend you a key from 8:00pm-12:00am Monday-Friday, 7:00am-12:00pm Saturday, and 10:00am-10:00pm Sunday. As a last resort, call BYU Police at (801) 422-2222 if neither of these offices is open.

What do I do if someone parks in my designated parking spot?

The parking stalls here are numbered for convenience only, so a car can't be ticketed or towed for parking in your spot. However, if the same car has been parked in your spot for more than a day, feel free to let us know so that we can try to help.

Please don't leave your own notes on others' cars. We have slips explaining the parking policy that you can place on cars -- let us know if you'd like one.

Also, remember that whoever is parked in your spot probably isn't on the ward Facebook page (and whomever they're visiting is probably busy with their guests, not checking the page themselves), so posting there rarely does any good.

Where should I tell my visitors to park?

Any unmarked stalls are designated as visitor spots. There are a few of these visitor spots in each parking lot, and the lot next to the Multi-Purpose Building is all visitor stalls.

Are the complete housing guidelines available online?

Yep! Go to this link, and then mouse over "Student Families" in the menu bar and click on the section of the guidelines that you'd like to read.

Can I drive in the fire lane when I move in/out?

Yes, you can. Let us know at least 24 hours in advance so we can unlock the gates, and be sure to get a permit from the office (located in the Multi-Purpose Building and open 8:00am-8:00pm Monday-Friday).
